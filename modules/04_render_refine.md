# Module 4 — Rendering & Refinement

## Purpose
Assemble the final output sections and ensure formatting consistency.

---

## Rendering Steps
1. Paper Summary — 1–2 paragraphs.
2. Section-by-Section Table — Markdown with columns: Section | Summary.
3. Expert Summary — retain detail.
4. Lay Summary — simplify and clarify.
5. Glossary — define technical terms.
6. Checks & Warnings — list missing/short sections or inconsistencies.

## Refinement Rules
- Keep voice consistent.
- No duplicated content across sections.
- Fix formatting issues.
